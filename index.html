About

My background is unusual on paper, but coherent in practice: support, teaching, troubleshooting, systems thinking, and communicating clearly under chaos. I like technical work that sits at the intersection of users, product behavior, and internal tooling.

What I do well

I’m strongest when the issue is ambiguous, the customer is frustrated, and the path forward is not obvious. I like isolating variables, validating assumptions, finding the real failure point, and turning noise into a clean escalation, workaround, or fix path.

What I’m building toward

I’m actively leveling up in Python, SQL, APIs, automation, and data workflows so I can move beyond traditional support and into more systems-oriented roles — support engineering, implementation, technical solutions, and internal tooling.
